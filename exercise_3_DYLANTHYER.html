Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Photo by Gian Cescon on Unsplash and Photo by Adam Bartoszewicz on Unsplash

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as ifyou are making an omelet)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate untilserved)
